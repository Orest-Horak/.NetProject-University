Since 1997 has been teaching at the Department of International Relations of Lviv State (now – National) University; in 1999-2000 delivered lectures in international business at the University of Kansas. In 2005 presented his Ph.D. thesis in the topic: “Mechanisms of reconciliation of the interests of transnational corporations with the strategic priorities of national economy”. Scholarly interests: multinational corporations, international relations, international economic relations and international business.<br />Over 50 scholarly works, including the textbook “Foreign Investments in the Economy of Ukraine”, a section of the textbook “International Relations: History, Theory, Economy”, translation of William R.Barnes’ and Larry C. Ledebur’s “New Regional Economies” into Ukrainian.<br />Winner of the All-Ukrainian competition “The New Intellect of Ukraine” among research fellows and teachers in the nomination “Economy” (2008).<br />Writes fiction, translates from English, German, Italian, Polish.</p></div></section><section class='section'><h2>Розклад</h2><div><p>Сторінка розкладу викладачів не знайдена!</p></div></section></article>
