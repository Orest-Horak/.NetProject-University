Organic chemist, Ph.D. in chemistry (“Arylation of furan derivatives by arenediazonium salts and transformations of reaction products”, Lviv, 1992). In 1978 she graduated faculty of chemistry Ivan Franko State University of Lviv. In 1975-78 she was working as assistant and in 1978-85 as engineer on department of organic chemistry. In 1980-84 she is postgraguate on department of organic chemistry. In 1985-96 she worked as associate researcher, researcher and senior researcher. Since 1996 she work as docent. O.
