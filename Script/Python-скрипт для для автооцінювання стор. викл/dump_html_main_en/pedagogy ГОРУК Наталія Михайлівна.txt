Currently she is a key staff member of the projects “Improving teacher education for applied learning in the field of vocational education” (Erasmus+ KA2, 2016-2018), “Enhancing higher education reform in Ukraine” (funded by the US Embassy in Ukraine, 2016-2017) and “European Quality of Educational Research for Empowering Educators in Ukraine” (Erasmus+, Jean Monet, 2017-2020).</p><p style="text-align: justify;"><p style="text-align: justify;"><p style="text-align: justify;"><p style="text-align: justify;"></div></section><section class='section'><h2>Schedule</h2><div><p>Page with teachers schedule not found!</p></div></section></article>
