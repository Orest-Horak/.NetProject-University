Born: December 20, 1978 in Lviv. In 1995, she graduated from Lviv Secondary School No. 63. 1995-2000 she was studying at the Faculty of Economics, Ivan Franko National University of Lviv. 2001-2003 she was studying the postgraduate course at Lviv State Financial Academy. Since 2001 she has been teaching at the Department of Economic Cybernetics at Lviv State Financial Academy.
