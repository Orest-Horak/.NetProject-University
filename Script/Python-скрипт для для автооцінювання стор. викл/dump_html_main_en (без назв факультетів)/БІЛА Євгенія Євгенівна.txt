Organic chemist, Ph.D. in chemistry (“Interaction of halogenostyrenes with diazonium salts”, Lviv, 1988), senior researcher (1993), docent (1999). In 1978 she graduated faculty of chemistry Ivan Franko State University of Lviv. In 1978-82 she was working as engineer, associate researcher and senior researcher. In 1982-87 she is postgraguate on department of organic chemistry. Since 1987 she worked as senior researcher, and since 1993 she work as docent on department of organic chemistry. E. Bila has about 60 papers, 7 patents and 7 textbooks.</p></div></section><section class='section'><h2>Schedule</h2><div><p>Page with teachers schedule not found!</p></div></section></article>
