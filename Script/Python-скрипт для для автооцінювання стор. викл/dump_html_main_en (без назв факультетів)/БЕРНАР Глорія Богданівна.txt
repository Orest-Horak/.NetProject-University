Since 2004 Ms Bernar has been working as a teacher of English in the English Department of the Faculty of Foreign Languages at Ivan Franko National University of Lviv.  From 2006 to 2009 she has been a post-graduate student, Department of English Philology. In 2012 Ms Bernar received a scientific degree of Candidate of Philological Sciences. Ms Bernar’s research was devoted to linguostylistic characteristics of English Drama of Absurd. In 2015 Gloria Bernar received an academic status of Associate Professor.</p></div></section><section class='section'><h2>Miscellaneous</h2><div><p><strong><i>Attended
