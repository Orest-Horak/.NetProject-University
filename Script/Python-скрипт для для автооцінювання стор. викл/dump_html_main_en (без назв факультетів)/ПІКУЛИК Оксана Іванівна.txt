<article class='content employee'><h1 class='page-title'>Oksana Pikulyk</h1><section class='general'><div class='info'></div></section><section class='section'><h2>Biography</h2><div><p>Candidate of Economic Sciences, associate professor of the Department of Economic Theory of the Faculty of Finance Management and Business, Ivan Franko National University of Lviv.</p><p style="text-align: justify;">Born: September 28, 1973 in Lviv. In 1990 she graduated from Lviv secondary school № 76. 1990-1995 she studied at the Faculty of Economics, Ivan Franko National University of Lviv. In 1995 she received a diploma with distinction in specialty: economy of enterprise, specialization: political economy. 1995-1998 she studied the postgraduate course at Ivan Franko National University of Lviv. Since 1998 &#8211; worked at the Department of Economics, Lviv State Institute of Physical Culture. In 1999 she defended her Ph.D. thesis on theme:&#8221;Equity in the conditions of the market transformation of the Ukrainian economy&#8221;. In 1999 she was awarded the scientific degree of the candidate of economic sciences in specialty: economic theory. Since 2000 – has been teaching at the Department of Economic Theory, Lviv State Financial Academy. In 2005 she was awarded the title of associate professor of the Department of Economic Theory.</p></div></section></article>
