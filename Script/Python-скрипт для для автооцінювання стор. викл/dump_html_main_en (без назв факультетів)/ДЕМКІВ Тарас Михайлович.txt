He got a diploma in Physics, the qualification of a physicist, a lecturer of Physics.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">From 1986 till 1989 he was an engineer and later on, research assistant of the Scientific Research Unit of Ivan Franko State University of Lviv.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">During the next 1989–1992 years Taras Demkiv was studying to become a post graduate at the Department of Physics of Semiconductors of Ivan Franko State University of Lviv.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">From 1992 till 1994 he was a scientific worker of Research and Development Department of Ivan Franko State University of Lviv.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">In 1994 Taras Demkiv defended the candidate thesis named <em>“Distribution and electronic properties of In<sub>4</sub>Se<sub>3 </sub>crystals”</em>. The scholar was awarded the academic degree of the Candidate of Physical and Mathematical Sciences specializing in Physics of Semiconductors and Dielectrics.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">During the next 1994–2007 years a senior lecturer and docent of the Department of Physics and mathematics at Academy of Veterinarian Medicine of Lviv (since 2003 at S. Z. Hzhytskyi National University of Veterenarian Medicine and Biotechnologies of Lviv).</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">In 2007 he came back to work at Ivan Franko National University of Lviv, where now, he is a docent of the Department of General Physics of the Faculty of Physics.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">Since 2010 – associate dean of the Faculty of Physics on scientific and methodological issues at Ivan Franko National University of Lviv.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">Since October, 2015 – acting head of the department of General Physics.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">In 2002, he was awarded with the academic status of the docent of the department of Physics and Mathematics.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">Taras Demkiv was a scientific supervisor of one thesis for the Candidate of Physical and Mathematical Sciences.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">He was an executive of state budget and economic contractual issues. The scholar published 104 scientific works and received 5 pathents of Ukraine for inventions and useful models.</span></p><p style="text-align: justify"><span style="color: #000000">Scientific – methodological work : a monograph, a manual, 5 tutorials, a methodical material.</span></p></div></section><section class='section'><h2>Teaching materials</h2><div><p><em><strong>Manual:</strong></em></p><p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="color: #000000;">Вакарчук С.
