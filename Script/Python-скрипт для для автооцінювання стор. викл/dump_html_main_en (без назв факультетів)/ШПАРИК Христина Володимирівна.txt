During 2010-2011 academic years, she had studied at the University of Vilnius (Vilnius, Lithuania) at the Faculty of Economics. In 2012, she graduated from the Ivan Franko National University of Lviv with a Master&#8217;s degree with distinction in international economics and received an international economist and translator qualification.</p><p>In 2012, she joined the postgraduate course at the faculty of law, Ivan Franko National University of Lviv, and graduated in 2014.</p><p>In 2012, she entered the postgraduate course at Ivan Franko National University of Lviv on the Department of International Economic Relations. During 2012-2013 academic year, she was studying at Mykolas Romeris University (Vilnius, Lithuania) at the Faculty of Finance and Economics. May &#8211; August 2014, she was studying at the United Nations Organization (New York, USA) at the Office of the Supreme Representative for the Least Developed Countries, Developing Countries and Landlocked, as well as Small Island States. January &#8211; April 2015 she was a researcher at the University of Alberta (Edmonton, Canada) at the Institute of Ukrainian Studies.</p><p>In October, 2015 she successfully passed her Ph.D. thesis on theme: &#8220;The Problem of Unequal Economic Development of the World Economy.&#8221; In December 2015 the scholar received scientific degree of the candidate of economic sciences on the specialty &#8220;World household&#8221; and &#8220;International economic relations&#8221;. Since September 2016 &#8211; a lecturer at the Department of Economic Theory at the Faculty of Finance Management and Business.</p></div></section></article>
